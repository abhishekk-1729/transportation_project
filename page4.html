Resilient Modulus

Test

The resilient modulus test provides a means of characterizing pavement construction materials including surface, base, and sub-base materials under a variety of temperatures and stress states that simulate the conditions in a pavement subjected to moving wheel loads. The test provides an excellent means for comparing the behavior of pavement construction materials under a variety of conditions (i.e., moisture, density, gradation, etc.) and stress states.
